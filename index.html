Welcome to Elgado Outpost

The forward operations base for the investigation into the abnormal monster behaviors from Elgado.

The Facilities of Elgado

The outpost has numerous facilities that a hunter can use to aid them in their quests.

Central Plaza

In the central plaza you can find the Oboro the merchant, Minayle the blacksmith, Chichae the quest receptionist, and your equipment box.

The Command Post

This here is the command post where we discuss the next steps for investigation on the monster.

Teashop

This where the residents of the outpost grab their dangos before heading out to hunt. Nearby the teashop is the anomly research lab.

Elgado Personals

Meet the faces of Elgado who make sure the outpust is up and running

Galleus

The commander of the Elgado Outpost. He is very stoic and a man of few words and is well respected by his peers.

Fiorayne

One of the many knights of Elgado that was brought along for the investigation. Can hold her own against the maryiad of large monsters that can be found near Elgado, and she is willing to put her well being at risk to complete her mission.

Bahari

The lead researcher in the investigation. Once he is invested in an research project he tends to get tunnel vision to the point where he would ignore his own safety.

Chichae

The princess of Elgado. She became a quest receptionist in order to help with the effort in invesitgating the return of the elder dragon Malzeno. Even though she is a princess she want to be treated as a normal worker at the outpost.

Oboro

A medical student that took the role of a merchant in order to pay for his tuition fee. He once study under Tadori and is waiting for a chance to resumes his studies with Tadori.

Minayle

The blacksmith of Elgado. She has the dream of becoming the best blacksmith in the world, and even recieve some lessons from Hamon the blacksmith of Kamura.

Arlow

The arena master, and the instructor of Fiorayne, Luchika, and Jay. Hail from the same town as Galleus and known him since their early days in the order.

Luchika

Part of the Royal Artillery squadron in the order. Outside of battle appear calm and collective, but once out on a hunt her personality turn more maniacal. This cause her peers to try advoid working with her out in the field.

Jay

A young knight in the order. He view Galleus as his role model in the order and what it means to be a knight.

Azuki

Azuki brought expertise in the making of dangos for the people at the outpost to enjoy while they are doing their work.

Tadori

A nomadic doctor as well the mentor to Oboro. He played a key role in finding a cure to the bloodblight disease cause by Malzeno.

Nagi

Nagi travelled from Kamura to the Elgado Outpost in order to handle the buddies for the Kamura hunters who are in the outpost.
